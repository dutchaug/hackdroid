Android 5 years!

On November 5th, 2012 Android celebrated its 5th birthday. To celebrate this, Google developer- and technology user group leaders in Europe announced the 'V Hack Android' hackathon (pronounced 'We Hack Android').

V Hack Android Dutch edition

The "V Hack Android" Netherlands hackathon was held in the weekend of Friday 14th - Sunday 16th of September 2012. The Google+ event page contains the transcript of the event.
